# Alloy Systems

## Titanium Alloys
- **Ti-6Al-4V (Ti64)**: Most widely used AM structural alloy. α+β microstructure. 
  YS ~900 MPa, UTS ~1000 MPa as-built L-PBF. Strong martensite (α') after rapid cooling.

## Nickel Superalloys
- **IN718**: Ni-Cr-Fe-Nb. γ'' (Ni₃Nb) + γ' strengthening. High-temperature aerospace use.
- **IN625**: Solid-solution strengthened. Better AM processability than IN718.

## Stainless Steel
- **SS316L**: Austenitic. Good corrosion resistance. Benchmark alloy for L-PBF studies.

## Aluminum Alloys
- **AlSi10Mg**: Near-eutectic. Most studied Al alloy in L-PBF. Fine Si network in as-built state.

## High-Entropy / Multi-Principal-Element Alloys (HEA/MPEA)
- **CoCrFeMnNi (Cantor)**: FCC, single-phase. Benchmark HEA. Good toughness at cryogenic T.
- **AlCoCrFeNi**: BCC/FCC dual-phase depending on Al content. Emerging AM research target.
- **RHEAs**: Refractory MPEAs (W, Mo, Ta, Nb, Hf). High-temperature applications.
